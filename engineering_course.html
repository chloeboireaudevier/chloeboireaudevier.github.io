Classes

Keep in mind that because I joined N7 in 2nd year, I did not follow semesters 5 and 6 courses!

Semester 3

  • Algorithms and Programming: Algorithms and Programming 1 & 2
  • Computer Architecture and Systems: Computer Architecture 1
  • Mathematics and Modeling: Mathematics for Computer Science
  • Human and Social Sciences: Communication, English, Sports (EPS)
  • Computer Project 1: Introductory Programming Project

Semester 4

  • Algorithms and Programming: Data Structures and Advanced Algorithms
  • Computer Architecture and Systems: Operating Systems and Networks 1
  • Mathematics and Modeling: Probability and Statistics
  • Human and Social Sciences: English, Communication, Sports (EPS)
  • Computer Project 2: Software Development Project
  • Engineering Tools: Introduction to Project Management

Semester 5

  • Systems and Networks: Operating Systems 2 and Advanced Networks
  • Programming: Object-Oriented Programming and Software Design
  • Databases: Modeling and SQL
  • Information Theory: Coding and Compression
  • Mathematics: Numerical Analysis and Statistics
  • Human and Social Sciences: English and Communication

Semester 6

  • Collective Project: Team Engineering Project
  • Computer Science & Telecom Options: Distributed Systems and Security, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, Signal and Image Processing
  • Human and Social Sciences: English, Communication, Sports (EPS)

Semester 7 — Software Systems Specialization

  • Soft & Human Skills: Professional Communication, English LV1 & LV2, Sports (EPS)
  • Local and Telecommunications Networks: Local Networks, Telecommunication Networks
  • Computer Architecture: Advanced Computer Architecture
  • Functional Programming and Compilation: Functional Programming, Language Translation
  • Concurrent and Communicating Systems: Concurrent Systems, Middleware, Distributed Data Project
  • Software and Systems Engineering: Model-Driven Engineering, Metaprogramming and Testing

Semester 8 — Software Systems Specialization

  • Soft & Human Skills: Communication, Foreign Language (LV2), Management & Entrepreneurship
  • Concurrent and Communicating Applications: OpenMP, Web Applications, Databases, Web Project
  • Formal Methods 1: Transition Systems, Static Analysis Verification
  • Emerging Programming Paradigms: Declarative Programming, Computability
  • Semantics and Language Translation: Language Semantics and Translation
  • Rendering and Deep Learning: Deep Learning, Computer Graphics

Semester 9 — Software Systems Specialization

  • Soft & Human Skills: Scientific English, Professional Communication
  • Real-Time Systems: Real-Time Scheduling, Real-Time Languages, IoT Arduino Project
  • Distributed Systems and Security: Distributed Systems, Cybersecurity, Parallel Computing
  • Refinement and Formal Methods: Formal Specification and Complex Systems Development
  • Information Access Interfaces: Semantic Web, Information Retrieval, Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), System Safety
  • Elective Course: Examples — Complex Graph Networks, Cybersecurity, Cloud & Big Data

Semester 10 — Final Year Internship

  • Graduation Internship: 6-month professional internship in a company or research lab
